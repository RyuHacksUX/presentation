My Angle

Having the abilitiy to 3D model might increase the chances of getting a job. This technology is important for a variety of applications, including product design, architecture, and animation. It allows designers and artists to create and visualize objects and scenes in a virtual environment, allowing them to experiment with different designs and make changes and adjustments quickly and easily.

3D modeling can be used to create realistic and detailed simulations, which can be helpful for testing and evaluating the performance of a product or design. It can create, visualize, and test their ideas in a virtual environment, improving the efficiency and effectiveness of the design process.
