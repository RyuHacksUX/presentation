My Angle

Creating a step-by-step process and documenting it when making a prototype is important. A well-defined process can help designers to plan and organize their work, ensuring that all necessary steps are completed in the correct order. It can provide a valuable reference for designers, as it can help them to remember the steps they took and the decisions they made during the prototype development process.

Documenting the process can also be useful for sharing and communicating with others who may be involved in the project, such as team members, stakeholders, or reviewers. It allows designers to help others to understand the design and development process, and can facilitate collaboration and feedback.
